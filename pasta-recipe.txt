Ingredients:
 - 2 pounds ground beef (or Turkey or Chicken. Can also be minced)
 - 2 cloves garlic, chopped
 - One 8-ounce can tomato sauce
 - 2 tablespoons chili powder
 - 1 teaspoon ground cumin
 - 1 teaspoon ground oregano
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1 teaspoon of basil
 - 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
 - One pound of broccoli
 - Pasta of choice (spaghetti, etc.)

Directions:
 1. Brown the ground beef in a large pot and drain off the excess fat.
 2. Add the tomato sauce, chili powder, cumin, oregano, basil, salt, and cayenne.
 3. Stir, then cover and simmer over low heat for one hour.
 4. Prepare the broccoli by steaming on stove top by adding 2 teaspoons of water in pot and heating for 5 to 6 minutes.
 5. Bring 3 cups of water to boil (Add salt optionally).
 6. Add in pasta and bring back to the boil uncovered for 8 minutes or to taste.
